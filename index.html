The Lantern in the Storm

3rd Quarter

The Lantern in the Storm

Characters

  • Eli
  • Villagers
  • Sailors

Setting

The story takes place in a small coastal village near the sea. The key events unfold during a violent night storm, with the lighthouse at the center of the action.

Conflict

A fierce storm strikes the village, and the lighthouse lamp suddenly goes out. Panic spreads as ships struggle to navigate through the dark waters, putting the sailors' lives at risk. Eli faces the challenge of climbing the steep hill to the lighthouse amid strong winds and heavy rain to fix the problem.

Moral Lesson

Even small actions or contributions can make a significant difference when others are in need or lost. Bravery isn't about being fearless - it's about acting to help others despite your fears.
